Hello...

I came to film not with noise but with stillness, from another life into this one, drawn by moments that speak without words.I watch. I listen. I tell quiet stories that hope to be felt.

My work has little to do with the things you see

The camera may capture images — but I try to film the in-betweens:The breath before a word, the warmth behind a shadow.
